About Us

Who we are..

Taco’s Not Bombs CC is a local non-profit in Corpus Christi, where we believe everyone deserves a good meal — no matter what. We stand against war and poverty, and our goal is simple: feed anyone who’s hungry. We meet every Sunday at 11:30 a.m. at Artesian Park in downtown Corpus Christi, where we cook, share food, and connect with our community. Everyone is welcome — come eat, volunteer, bring food, or just hang out! Donations, food or monetary, are always appreciated and go directly toward feeding people in need. We love showing how the community comes together, so we share photos and updates of what’s been donated and how it’s being used. Join us in spreading love, tacos, and community — because together, we can make a difference.
